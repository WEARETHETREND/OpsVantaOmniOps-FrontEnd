# Professional Conduct Policy

**© 2026 OpsVanta LLC — All Rights Reserved.**

This policy applies to all authorized OpsVanta personnel, approved contractors,
and partners with access to this repository and related systems.

---

## Our Standard

OpsVanta LLC expects all team members and authorized collaborators to conduct
themselves professionally, respectfully, and in alignment with the company's
values and obligations.

## Expected Behavior

All authorized contributors are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and respectfully at all times
- Protect the confidentiality of proprietary information and trade secrets
- Raise concerns or disagreements through appropriate internal channels
- Respect the time, expertise, and work of colleagues
- Act in the best interest of OpsVanta LLC and its products

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct of any kind
- Unauthorized disclosure of proprietary information, source code, or internal documentation
- Sabotage of systems, code, or processes
- Misrepresentation of work, authorship, or credentials
- Use of company resources for unauthorized purposes

## Reporting

Concerns regarding professional conduct should be reported directly to:

📧 **Email:** contact@opsvanta.com

All reports are treated with discretion. Substantiated violations may result in
access revocation, contract termination, or other appropriate action.

## Confidentiality Reminder

All authorized personnel are bound by a signed NDA and the terms described in
[ACCESS_POLICY.md](./ACCESS_POLICY.md). Conduct obligations survive termination
of access or employment.
