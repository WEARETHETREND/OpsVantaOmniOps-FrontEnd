# Quick Start Guide

**Get started with OpsVanta in just 5 minutes!**

## What is OpsVanta?

OpsVanta is an AI-powered website builder that lets you create stunning, professional websites in seconds. Simply describe what you want, and our AI will build it for you.

## Your First Website in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Sign Up (30 seconds)

1. Go to [opsvanta.com](https://opsvanta.com)
2. Click **"Get Started Free"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Create Your First Project (1 minute)

1. Click **"New Project"** from your dashboard
2. Choose a project type:
   - 🎯 Landing Page
   - 🌐 Business Website
   - 📱 Portfolio
   - 🛍️ E-commerce Site
   - 📰 Blog

### Step 3: Describe Your Website (2 minutes)

Tell the AI what you want:

```
I need a modern landing page for my coffee shop. 
Include sections for our menu, locations, and contact form.
Use warm brown colors and include photos of coffee.
```

**Tips for better results:**
- Be specific about content sections
- Mention your preferred colors/style
- Include your target audience
- Specify any must-have features

### Step 4: Let AI Build It (30 seconds)

1. Click **"Generate with AI"**
2. Watch as AI creates your website in real-time
3. Preview the generated design

### Step 5: Customize & Publish (1 minute)

1. Use the visual editor to make any changes
2. Click **"Publish"** when ready
3. Your site is live instantly at `yourproject.opsvanta.com`
