# Account Setup Guide

Complete guide to creating and configuring your OpsVanta account.

## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Process

1. **Visit OpsVanta**
   - Go to [opsvanta.com](https://opsvanta.com)
   - Click **"Get Started Free"** or **"Sign Up"**

2. **Choose Sign-Up Method**
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - GitHub account
   - Microsoft account

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Email Sign-Up

```
Email: your.email@example.com
Password: [8+ characters, include letters & numbers]
Full Name: Your Name
Company: Your Company (optional)
```

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one letter
- At least one number
- Special characters recommended

### Social Sign-Up

**Benefits:**
- Faster sign-up process
- No password to remember
- Automatic account verification

**Supported Providers:**
- Google
- GitHub
- Microsoft

## Account Configuration

### Profile Settings

Access: **Dashboard → Settings → Profile**

#### Basic Information

- **Full Name** - Your display name
- **Email Address** - Primary contact email
- **Profile Picture** - Upload a photo or avatar
- **Job Title** - Your role (optional)
- **Company Name** - Your organization (optional)
- **Phone Number** - Contact number (optional)

#### Preferences

- **Language** - Interface language (English, Spanish, French, German)
- **Time Zone** - Your local time zone
- **Date Format** - MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY
- **Theme** - Light, Dark, or Auto

### Security Settings

Access: **Dashboard → Settings → Security**

#### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

**Highly Recommended** for account security.

1. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
2. Choose authentication method:
   - Authenticator app (recommended)
   - SMS text messages
   - Email codes
3. Scan QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

**Supported Authenticator Apps:**
- Google Authenticator
- Authy
- 1Password
- Microsoft Authenticator

#### Password Management

**Change Password:**
1. Go to Security Settings
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password twice
5. Click **"Update Password"**

**Forgot Password:**
1. Go to login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check email for reset link
5. Create new password

#### Active Sessions

View and manage active login sessions:

- Current device and location
- Last activity time
- Ability to revoke access

**Security Best Practice:** Regularly review and revoke unused sessions.

### Notification Settings

Access: **Dashboard → Settings → Notifications**

#### Email Notifications

Configure what emails you receive:

- ✅ **Project Updates** - Changes to your projects
- ✅ **Team Activity** - Team member actions
- ✅ **Billing & Invoices** - Payment notifications
- ✅ **System Updates** - Platform announcements
- ✅ **Tips & Resources** - Learning materials
- ✅ **Marketing** - Product news and offers

#### In-App Notifications

Control dashboard notifications:

- Build completions
- Publishing status
- Team mentions
- Comment replies
- Workflow triggers

#### Notification Frequency

- Real-time
- Hourly digest
- Daily digest
- Weekly summary

### Billing Information

Access: **Dashboard → Settings → Billing**

#### Payment Methods

Add credit cards or PayPal:

1. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
2. Enter card details or connect PayPal
3. Set as default payment method (optional)
4. Click **"Save"**

**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- PayPal
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plans)

#### Billing Address

Required for invoices and tax calculations:

- Company name
- Street address
- City, State, ZIP/Postal code
- Country
- VAT/Tax ID (if applicable)

#### Invoices & Receipts

- View all past invoices
- Download PDF receipts
- Export billing history

### Team Settings

Access: **Dashboard → Settings → Team**

See [Team Management](collaboration.md) for detailed information.

## Account Management

### Changing Email Address

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Click **"Change Email"**
3. Enter new email address
4. Verify new email
5. Confirm change

### Deactivating Account

**Temporary deactivation:**
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click **"Deactivate Account"**
3. Confirm deactivation
4. Reactivate anytime by logging in

### Deleting Account

**⚠️ Permanent action - cannot be undone**

1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click **"Delete Account"**
3. Export your data (recommended)
4. Enter password to confirm
5. Account deleted within 30 days

**Data Retention:**
- Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Project data: Deleted after 30 days
- Personal info: Deleted immediately
