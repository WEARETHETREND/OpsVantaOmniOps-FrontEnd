# Account Setup Video Tutorial
**Duration:** 2 minutes
**Target Audience:** New users
**Prerequisites:** None

## Learning Objectives
- Create an OpsVanta account
- Verify email address
- Configure basic settings

## Script

### Introduction (0:00-0:15)
In this video, we'll walk through setting up your OpsVanta account step by step.

### Section 1: Sign Up Process (0:15-0:45)
Navigate to opsvanta.com and click "Get Started Free" in the top right corner.

You'll see options to sign up with:
- Email and password
- Google account
- GitHub account

Choose your preferred method and fill in the required information.

### Section 2: Email Verification (0:45-1:15)
After signing up, check your inbox for the verification email from OpsVanta.

Click the verification link in the email. You'll be redirected to your dashboard, confirming your account is now active.

### Section 3: Initial Configuration (1:15-1:45)
Let's configure your profile:
1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Select "Settings"
3. Update your display name
4. Add a profile picture (optional)
5. Set your preferred language and timezone

### Conclusion (1:45-2:00)
Your account is now ready! In the next video, we'll build your first website in just 5 minutes.
