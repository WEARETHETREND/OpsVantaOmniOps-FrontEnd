# Building Your First Website in 5 Minutes
**Duration:** 5 minutes
**Target Audience:** Beginners
**Prerequisites:** OpsVanta account created

## Learning Objectives
- Create a new project
- Use AI to generate a website
- Publish your first website

## Script

### Introduction (0:00-0:30)
Welcome back! Today, we're going to build your first professional website using OpsVanta's AI in just 5 minutes.

By the end of this video, you'll have a complete, live website ready to share with the world.

### Section 1: Creating a New Project (0:30-1:00)
From your dashboard, click the "New Project" button.

You'll see several project type options:
- Landing Page - Perfect for product launches
- Business Website - Multi-page professional site
- Portfolio - Showcase your work
- Blog - Share your thoughts

For this tutorial, let's select "Landing Page."

### Section 2: Describing Your Website (1:00-2:30)
Now comes the magic - telling the AI what you want.

Here's an example prompt:
"Create a modern landing page for a coffee subscription service. Include a hero section with sign-up form, benefits of subscription, customer testimonials, pricing plans, and contact information. Use warm brown and cream colors."

Notice how we:
- Specified the business type
- Listed the sections we need
- Mentioned our color preferences
- Described the target audience

### Section 3: AI Generation (2:30-3:00)
Click "Generate with AI" and watch the magic happen.

The AI is now:
- Creating your layout
- Choosing colors and fonts
- Arranging sections
- Optimizing for mobile

This usually takes 30-60 seconds.

### Section 4: Preview and Customize (3:00-4:00)
Great! Your website is generated. Let's preview it.

You can:
- Click any text to edit it
- Replace placeholder images with your own
- Adjust colors using the color picker
- Reorder sections by dragging

Try clicking the headline and changing it to your actual business name.

### Section 5: Publishing (4:00-4:45)
Happy with your website? Let's publish it!

Click the "Publish" button in the top right.

Choose your publishing option:
- Free subdomain: yourname.opsvanta.com
- Custom domain: Connect your own domain (we'll cover this in a later video)

For now, let's use the free subdomain. Enter your desired name and click "Publish Now."

### Conclusion (4:45-5:00)
Congratulations! Your website is now live and accessible to anyone on the internet.

Copy your URL and share it with friends, or post it on social media.

In the next video, we'll explore the visual editor and advanced customization options.
